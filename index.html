Frequently asked

Where is my data stored?

Entirely on your device. Lynx has no account and no servers — nothing is uploaded anywhere. See the Privacy Policy.

How do I back up or move my data?

Settings → Data → Save backup writes a single backup file you control (save it to Files, iCloud Drive, etc.). To restore — on a new phone or after reinstalling — use Restore and pick that file. Photos live on the device and aren't inside the backup.

My reminders aren't showing up.

Make sure notifications are allowed: iOS Settings → Lynx → Notifications. Background refresh is best-effort — iOS decides when it runs — so the reminder content is most reliable while you open the app regularly.

What is Lynx Full?

A one-time, optional unlock (no subscriptions) that raises the bond limit and adds automatic backups, widget styling, bond goals, and CSV export. The core experience is free, and your existing bonds are never locked.

How do I delete my data?

Delete any bond from its detail screen, or remove everything at once by deleting the app — since all data is local, that erases it completely.
